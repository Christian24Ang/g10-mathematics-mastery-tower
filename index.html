Rules

How to Play

3 Hearts Per Floor

Each floor starts with 3 hearts. A correct answer gives +1 score. A wrong answer removes 1 heart but the run continues. Game Over happens only when all 3 hearts are lost on the same floor.

Four Towers

The Grand Mastery Tower has 30 floors representing the 30 weeks of the Grade 10 Mathematics BOW. Each Term Tower has 10 floors representing the 10 weeks of that term. Choose a tower on the homepage before entering your details.

Quit Button

Use Quit Run to voluntarily stop your attempt. It does not count as a wrong answer.

Practice Mode

Choose any term and week, answer 10 review questions, see feedback and explanations, then review missed items. Practice Mode does not affect Firebase or the leaderboard.

Leaderboard

Students are ranked by floor reached, sublevel reached, highest score, then fastest time. This rewards both progress and accuracy.
